First Time?

What To Expect.

Showing up to a new church can feel like a lot. Here's the heads-up so you walk in ready.

Step 01

When You Arrive

A greeter at the door will welcome you and walk you to the sanctuary. There's no dress code — dress respectfully and come as you are.

Step 02

During Service

Expect Spirit-filled worship and the Word preached with clarity. Service runs about 2 hours.

Step 03

After Service

Stop by the welcome table. Grab coffee, meet a leader, get prayer, or ask questions. We'd love to know you came.
