Where Your Gift Goes

Sown Into Kingdom Work.

Every gift given to 2820 Church helps move the mission forward. As a growing house church preparing to move into a permanent building, our giving is focused on building a strong foundation for ministry, outreach, and long-term community impact.

No one at 2820 Church is serving for a paycheck. Our pastor, musicians, leaders, and volunteers serve out of love for the Lord and a desire to see people come to Christ. Every act of service is given freely, and every dollar is stewarded with care, prayer, and purpose.

65%

Building Fund

Purchasing and preparing a future church home where we can gather, worship, disciple families, serve the community, and expand the work God has called us to do.

20%

Ministry & Church Needs

Children's ministry supplies, worship equipment, teaching materials, outreach tools, communion supplies, event materials, and the day-to-day needs of a growing church.

15%

Outreach & Care

Evangelism, acts of service, helping families and individuals in need, community outreach, and showing the love of Christ in practical ways.

"And all who believed were together and had all things in common... distributing the proceeds to all, as any had need." — Acts 2:44–45

Frequently Asked

Giving Questions.

Q

Is online giving secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and never store your card details on our servers.

Q

Will I get a giving statement?

Absolutely. Annual statements are emailed each January for your tax records.

Q

Can I designate where my gift goes?

Yes — choose general giving, missions, building fund, or special offerings on the giving page.

Q

What if I have other questions?

Email giving@2820church.org and we'll get back to you within 1-2 business days.
