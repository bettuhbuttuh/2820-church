About 2820

Faith. Fire. Family.

A Bible-believing Christian church — built on the Gospel, kept by the Spirit, and called to make disciples who make disciples.

Our Story

Built On A Burden For Lost Souls.

2820 Church began with something simple: prayer, the Word of God, and a burden to see lives changed through Jesus Christ.

The Beginning

A House Of Prayer.

Before there is a building, a stage, or a full ministry schedule, there is a house filled with people seeking God together. That is the heart of who we are. We believe the church is not built first by walls, lights, or programs, but by people who gather in faith, love one another, and obey the Word of God.

Right now, 2820 Church is growing as a house church while preparing for the next step: moving into a permanent building where we can serve more people, reach more families, and build stronger ministries for the community.

Today

A Family On Mission.

Today, 2820 Church gathers every Sunday at 11:00 AM as a Bible-believing Christian church committed to prayer, discipleship, worship, evangelism, and service.

We are a family of believers who want to see people know Jesus, grow in faith, and walk in the purpose God has for their lives. Our church is multigenerational, community-minded, and focused on making disciples who live out their faith beyond Sunday service.

From children's ministry to men's fellowship, from prayer gatherings to outreach, from community service to evangelism, every part of who we are exists for one reason: to preach Christ, make disciples, and see lives transformed by Him.

Our Heart

Built On The Word.

2820 Church is a Bible-believing Christian church. We believe the Word of God is our foundation, our authority, and our guide for life.

We are not trying to chase trends or build a church around popularity. We want to build according to Scripture. That means we are committed to sound teaching, sincere worship, fervent prayer, genuine fellowship, and a lifestyle that reflects Jesus Christ.

Our desire is simple: that every person who comes connected to 2820 Church would encounter God, understand His Word, and grow into a faithful disciple of Jesus.

Our Community

Serving Beyond The Walls.

Even as a house church, we believe the mission of the church reaches far beyond where we gather.

2820 Church is committed to being present in the community through acts of service, outreach, evangelism, prayer, and compassion. We want to be the kind of church that does not just invite people in, but also goes out to meet people where they are.

Whether that means helping families, encouraging youth, supporting neighbors, praying with those in need, or creating opportunities for people to experience the love of Christ, we believe the church should be visible, active, and useful in the community.

Where We're Going

A Vision For More.

We are thankful for where God has us now, but we believe this is only the beginning.

As 2820 Church prepares to move into a building, our vision is to create a place where families can worship, children can grow, leaders can be developed, the community can be served, and the Gospel can be preached with clarity and power.

We are building for Sunday worship, but also for everyday impact. We see a church that serves the neighborhood, trains disciples, supports families, develops ministries, and becomes a place of hope for the community.

What We Believe

Statement Of Faith.

We hold to the Bible as the Word of God and the foundation of our faith — centered on Jesus Christ, strengthened by the Holy Spirit, and lived out through the local church.

01

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God — sufficient for faith and practice.

02

One True God

There is one God, eternally existing in three persons — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

03

Jesus Christ

Jesus Christ is fully God and fully man, born of a virgin, crucified for our sins, raised bodily, and coming again.

04

Salvation

Salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone — not the result of works, that no one may boast.

05

The Holy Spirit

The Holy Spirit indwells every believer, empowers them for ministry, and produces the fruit of Christlikeness.

06

The Church

The Church is the body of Christ — called to worship, discipleship, fellowship, ministry, and the Great Commission.

07

Resurrection

All people will be resurrected — believers to eternal life with Christ, and unbelievers to eternal separation from Him.

08

The Great Commission

We are commanded by Christ to make disciples of all nations, baptizing and teaching them to obey everything He commanded.
