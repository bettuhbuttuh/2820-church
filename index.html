About 2820

A Church Built on Faith, Fire, and Purpose.

At 2820 Church, we exist to glorify God by leading people into a life-changing relationship with Jesus Christ. We believe in the power of the Holy Spirit, the authority of God's Word, and the call to make disciples who make disciples. You're not just attending — you're becoming part of a family on mission.

Our Mission

We Preach Christ

We boldly proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ with clarity, conviction, and compassion.
